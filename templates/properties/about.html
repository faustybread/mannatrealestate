Our story

Two decades of Rohtak,
one relationship at a time.

MR Estates began with a simple idea — that buying property in Rohtak should not require a maze of brokers, unverified plots, or opaque paperwork. Under Managing Director Manish Garg’s stewardship, we’ve grown into a partner-led firm known for saying no more often than yes.

We list only the properties we would purchase ourselves. Every title is checked; every listing carries the founder’s sign-off. That standard is why our clients — end-users, investors, and NRIs alike — return to us for their second, third, and fourth transactions.

Why Choose Us

Transparency,
on the record.

Rohtak has no shortage of estate agents. What sets MR Estates apart is a written commitment to legal compliance, verified titles, and founder-signed diligence on every deal.

{# ── Feature 1 · RERA ── #}

RERA registered

Every listing complies with the Haryana Real Estate Regulatory Authority — Panchkula. You’ll see the registration number on the paperwork before a single rupee changes hands.

{# ── Feature 2 · Founder-led ── #}

Founder-led team

A partner reviews every listing before it’s published. No junior sales agents, no chase-the-lead call centre — every conversation is with someone accountable.

{# ── Feature 3 · Local expertise ── #}

Rohtak, top to bottom

Twenty years of on-ground presence across Suncity, Omaxe City, and the numbered sectors. We know which builders are worth trusting and which plots to walk away from.

{# ── Feature 4 · Title diligence ── #}

Verified paperwork

Title deed, mutation, sanctioned plan, and encumbrance certificate — each document is checked by our in-house counsel before we accept a listing.

{# ── Feature 5 · No broker chains ── #}

Direct pricing

No middleman chains, no phantom commissions. Every quoted price is what the seller has authorised in writing.
